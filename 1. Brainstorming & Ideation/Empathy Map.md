# Empathy Map

## Says

- "I want to sound natural, not robotic."
- "I do not want awkward silence at the start of a conversation."

## Thinks

- "What if I ask something irrelevant?"
- "I need something fast before I walk into the event."

## Does

- Reads about the event
- Looks for common talking points
- Tries to remember personal interests that connect to the topic

## Feels

- Nervous before events
- More confident when suggestions are specific and practical
