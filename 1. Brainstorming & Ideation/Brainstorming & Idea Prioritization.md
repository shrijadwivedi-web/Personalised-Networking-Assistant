# Brainstorming & Idea Prioritization

## Ideas Considered

- Generic chat assistant
- Event-based conversation starter generator
- Fact-checking helper
- Personal networking history tracker

## Final Direction

The strongest idea combined the event-based starter generator with profile-based personalization,
fact checking, and saved history.

## Why It Was Chosen

- Useful before real networking conversations
- Clear AI and product fit
- Easy to demonstrate with multiple features in one app
